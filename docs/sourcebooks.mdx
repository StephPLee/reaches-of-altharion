# Sourcebooks

This page lists the sourcebooks and sourcebook-style supplements players are most likely to reference when building characters.

## Policy

- Official Wizards of the Coast Dungeons & Dragons books are allowed unless staff says otherwise.
- Non-official partnered books are allowed unless they appear in the explicit not allowed list below.
- The banned partnered items listed below remain not allowed even if a player owns them on D&D Beyond.

This page focuses on rulebooks, campaign settings, player-option books, and other sourcebook-style supplements. It does not try to catalog every adventure module.

## Notes On Editions

- `5e` means legacy 2014-rules content.
- `5.5e` means 2024-rules content.
- `Mixed` means the product appears to support both 2014 and 2024 content, or the marketplace listing is not cleanly one or the other.
